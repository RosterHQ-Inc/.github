# RosterHQ Data & Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** July 1, 2026

RosterHQ is committed to protecting the privacy and security of athletes, brands, and other users. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices users have regarding their data.

## 1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information:

- Account information (name, email, phone number, school, business information)
- Athlete profile information (sport, position, social media metrics, NIL experience, photos)
- Brand profile information (company details, campaigns, billing information)
- Usage data (device information, browser type, IP address, pages visited, session activity)
- Payment information processed securely through third-party payment providers

## 2. How We Use Your Data

We use collected information to:

- Operate and improve the RosterHQ platform
- Match athletes with brands
- Process payments and transactions
- Verify user identities
- Provide customer support
- Send important service communications
- Improve security and prevent fraud
- Analyze platform performance

## 3. Information Sharing

RosterHQ does not sell personal information.

Information may be shared:

- Between athletes and brands when applying for opportunities
- With trusted service providers (hosting, payment processing, analytics)
- When required by law or legal process
- To protect the rights, safety, or security of users or RosterHQ

## 4. Data Security

RosterHQ uses commercially reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards including:

- Encrypted connections
- Access controls
- Secure authentication
- Continuous monitoring

## 5. Data Retention

We retain information only as long as necessary to:

- Operate the platform
- Comply with legal obligations
- Resolve disputes
- Enforce agreements

## 6. User Rights

Users may:

- Access their personal information
- Update inaccurate information
- Delete their account (subject to legal retention requirements)
- Request a copy of their personal data where applicable

## 7. Cookies & Analytics

RosterHQ may use cookies and similar technologies to:

- Remember preferences
- Maintain sessions
- Measure performance
- Improve user experience

## 8. Children's Privacy

RosterHQ is not intended for children under 13. Where athletes are minors, applicable parental consent and legal requirements must be satisfied.

## 9. Third-Party Services

Links and integrations with third-party platforms (such as payment processors or social media services) are governed by those providers' privacy policies.

## 10. Policy Updates

RosterHQ may update this policy periodically. Material changes will be communicated through the platform or by email where appropriate.

## 11. Contact

For privacy-related questions or requests, contact RosterHQ through the contact information listed on the platform.
